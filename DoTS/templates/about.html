What does it do?

This is a free and easy to use web service for predicting endocrine disruption potential of molecules. This server performs docking is to 18 integrated, thoroughly validated crystal structures of 14 different nuclear receptors.

It uses AutoDock Vina.

How to use it?

Click on prediction and either draw a structure or submit SMILES.

How to interpret the results?

The 18 structures integrated in Endocrine Disruptome are carefully selected from 103 structures. All structures and protocols are validated. Users are advised to consult the receptors' sub-pages to check the performance of each model.
