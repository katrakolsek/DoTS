About Endocrine Disruptome

Endocrine Disruptome and DoTS are a result of work of Katra Kolšek and Samo Turk.

This work is supported by Faculty of Pharmacy, University of Ljubljana, Slovenia and National Institute of Chemistry, Slovenia.

The server running Endocrine Disruptome is kindly hosted by Laboratory for Biocomputing and Bioinformatics, National Institute of Chemistry. Special thanks for this goes to prof. Janez Mavri and Deni Bačić.

This is a free and easy to use web service for predicting endocrine disruption potential of molecules. This server performs docking to 18 integrated, thoroughly validated crystal structures of 14 different nuclear receptors.
